NIUSAT

NIUSAT is an Indian University/Academic Institute satellite from Noorul Isalm University in Tamil Nadu State, launched by PSLV-C38.

KALAMSAT

Kalamsat-V2, a student payload is first to use fourth stage (PS4) of the PSLV as an orbital platform. The satellite was taken to its designated orbit by PSLV-C44.

INSPIRESAT

INSPIREsat-1 from Indian Institute of Space Science & Technology (IIST) in association with Laboratory of Atmospheric & Space Physics at University of Colorado, Boulder
